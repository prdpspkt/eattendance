Shift Information
Understanding Shift Settings:
  • Start/End Time: Define when employees should start and end work
  • Late Grace Period: Minutes allowed after start time before marking as late
  • Early Exit Tolerance: Minutes allowed before end time without penalty
  • Break Duration: Duration of lunch/break breaks (subtracted from working hours)
Example: If shift is 9:00-17:00 with 60min break:
- Employee works 8:00-17:00 → Not late (within grace period)
- Employee works 9:30-17:00 → Late by 30 minutes
- Working hours = (End - Start) - Break duration
